Dr. Vivek Kamat

Assistant Research Professor · Vanderbilt University

Research Focus

AI-enabled biosensing, additive manufacturing, and intelligent healthcare systems. My research focuses on wearable biosensors, smart wound-care systems, and data-driven platforms for real-time clinical monitoring.

Research Vision

The future of healthcare lies in the seamless integration of materials, electronics, sensing architectures, and artificial intelligence. My work develops unified systems that enable real-time diagnostics and decision-making across clinical and engineering environments.

Key Areas

  • Wearable biosensors and wound monitoring
  • AI for sensing and diagnostics
  • Additive manufacturing and plasma-assisted printing
  • Biocompatible materials and lab-on-chip systems

Selected Publications

  • Electrochemical detection of testosterone using MIP-based sensing platform
  • AI-enabled detection of DNA damage in comet assay
  • Microfluidic reactor-assisted nanoparticle synthesis
  • Smart bandage for wound monitoring
